Furniture Storage

Make sure you have a place to store your patio furniture during the winter to prevent it from getting
damaged. If you have limited storage space, buy pieces that can stack or fold. Be sure to cover sets
that can’t be moved from outside and bring all cushions, planters and accessories inside.

The Best Outdoor Materials

OLEFIN

A synthetic fabric that resists fading,
mildew and moisture.

SYNTHETIC WICKER

Durable, easy to clean and looks
like real wicker.

ALUMINUM

Durable, rust-free and lightweight
enough to be moved around easily.

POLYESTER

A synthetic fabric that must be treated
with a protective coating to resist
moisture and fading.

EUCALYPTUS WOOD

Strong, dense, weather-resistant wood
that can be oiled to hold its colour
or left to fade to silver.

Steel

Heavy, durable and often
powder-coated on outdoor products
to prevent rusting.
