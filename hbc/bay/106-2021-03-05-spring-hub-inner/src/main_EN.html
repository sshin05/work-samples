1/ ABOVE THE KEYBOARD

Working from home means half your outfit works twice as hard. The solution: fabulous fabrics, puffed sleeves and dynamic collars that need to be seen.

2/ MATCHY MATCHY

Not too long ago, “matchy” was synonymous with “tacky.” Not anymore. Now, coordinated combos are equal parts effortless and polished.

3/ LADIES WHO LOUNGE

Home style has officially been perfected. Matching lounge sets, jumpsuits and luxe everyday leggings worn with tailored or athleisure pieces is *chef’s kiss.*

4/ dress on the bright side

Do a little happy dance in bright and uplifting colour palettes. Whether you’re mixing and matching or sporting one big bold hue, vivid colours spark joy.

5/ Second Skins

Celebrate your form in body-con layers, and mix knockout colours and prints for a serious—but not too serious—spring statement. It’s finally time to bust out those biker shorts.

6/ Neutral Naturals

Layered whites and off-whites paired with gladiator sandals or leather slides have a very “fizzy drink with an ocean view” vibe. We can dream.

7/ SUPREME STAPLES

In a topsy-turvy world, there’s nothing more comforting than timeless favourites: the just-right tee, that sweater that gives you feels, and a classic blazer that elevates it all.

8/ The Easy Dress

Elegance and ultimate comfort in one—a revelation! Your everyday dress, but softer and easier. Pair with a carryall bag for an of-the-moment look. Go ahead, flatter yourself.

9/ This Season’s Knit Girl

Warmer weather is no match for comfy-cool knits. From subtle weaves to more detailed designs, ribbed knits to crochets; as a dress, skirt or short, knits are a sunny new staple.

10/ SAGE ADVICE

Evoking both serenity and newness, it’s easy to see why this shade of green is trending. Pair with several deeper greens for a modern take on a tonal look.
