The 5 Types of Countertop Ovens

1.

Air Fry and Convection Ovens

This type of oven combines the features of an air fryer and convection oven, which means you can cook meals even faster and more efficiently.

Perfect for: Crispy chicken wings, sweet potato fries, roast beef, cinnamon rolls

Shop Air Fry & Convection Ovens

2.

Convection Ovens

With the help of a fan that circulates hot air,convection ovens tend to cook food quicker and more evenly than a standard toaster oven.

Perfect for: Roast chicken, pizza, chocolate chip cookies, apple pie

Shop Convection Ovens

3.

Air Fryers

Uses the convection effect which circulates hot air inside the oven. Makes food crispy like a deep fryer, but with virtually no oil.

Perfect for: French fries, chicken tenders, egg rolls, kale chips

Shop Air Fryers

4.

Toaster Ovens

A mini version of your full-size oven, toaster ovens are great for broiling, baking, heating and, of course, toasting.

Perfect for: Toast, tuna melt, frozen pizza, baked fish

Shop Toaster Ovens

5.

Microwave Ovens

Microwaves have come a long way. Many can do much more than just reheat food like steaming and browning. Some microwaves can even air fry.

Perfect for: Baked potatoes, poached eggs, steamed vegetables
