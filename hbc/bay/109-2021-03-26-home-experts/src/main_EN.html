3 Easy Cooking Tips

1.

Mise en Place. Measure, chop and prep your ingredients beforehand so cooking can be all fun.

2.

A Sharp Knife. Create beautifully sliced ingredients and stay safer in the kitchen (dull is dangerous!)

3.

A Tidy Work Area. Clear and stabilize your cutting board and have all your tools within reach.

3 Cooking Dont's

  1. Don’t cut corners. If you’re pressed for time, choose a simpler recipe
  2. When searing meat, fish and seafood, don’t use a non-stick pan. Just add a little oil and be patient. Your perfectly seared item will lift off when it’s ready
  3. Don’t cross contaminate. Clean or change cutting boards after contact with raw meat or fish. Plus, always wash the vegetables (and your hands!).
