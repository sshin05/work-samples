3 Easy Design Tips

  1. “Lighting can make or break even the best
    interiors. Choose fixtures and pieces that are
    unique and bold to create an eye-catching
    moment that guides visual focus upward.”
  2. “Create visual layers on your walls with
    striking patterns, colours or designs. Try a
    bold accent wall or cover every wall in your
    space with a chic wallpaper, turning the
    surface into a work of art.”
  3. “Choose a paint hue that pairs well with your
    furnishings and fixtures. Then, apply it to
    your walls, millwork and even ceilings.
    You will be surprised by the power that a new
    splash of paint will have on your space.”

3 Design Dont's

  1. “Don’t fold or crease your decorative
    pillows. Casually arrange them for a
    clean and sophisticated look.”
  2. “Don’t buy an area rug that is too small for
    your space. Make sure that your furniture
    can be placed on the rug with adequate
    space between pieces.”
  3. “Don’t feel obliged to buy a matching
    furniture set. Try creating your own
    mix of unique pieces to create visual
    interest and a cohesive flow.”
