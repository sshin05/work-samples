3 Easy Baking Tips

  1. Make sure your oven is fully preheated before you bake.
  2. Ingredients of a like temperature blend together better, so use the temperature of your butter as a guide: If a recipe calls for room temperature butter, your other ingredients should be the same, and if the recipe calls for cold butter, then your milk, eggs, etc., should also be cold.
  3. I like to line my pans with parchment paper, which makes cleanup a snap. If you don’t use parchment, remember that even a non-stick pan should be lightly greased.

3 Baking Don'ts

  1. Don’t start baking until you’ve read the recipe all the way through. This helps prevent getting caught without an ingredient, tool or time.
  2. Don’t frost a warm cake. Make sure that cake has cooled completely to room temperature so that the frosting will adhere (and not melt).
  3. Don’t forget the music. Your favourite playlist can get you into a relaxed mood for baking.
