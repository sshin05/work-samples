SPRING SOLUTIONS

Switch Off All the Lights

When nearing bedtime, be gentle on your eyes by avoiding bright lights as they inhibit the production of the melatonin hormone that aids in sleep. To ensure ultimate darkness as you sleep, opt for thicker blinds or use a silk eye mask.

Blackout Curtains →    Sleep Masks →   

Avoid Looking at Screens

Digital devices emit blue light that can strain your eyes. Instead of looking at your phone before bed, read a book or listen to a podcast or journal.

Books & Journals →   

Calm Your Senses with Aromatherapy

Essential oils like clary sage, peppermint, sweet marjoram and chamomile can relieve insomnia and anxiety.

Essential Oils & Diffusers →   

Wear Pajamas to Bed

High-quality pajamas in soft, breathable fabrics like cotton let you move freely during the night and keep cool until morning.
