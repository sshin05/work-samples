To return a product purchased through our Marketplace:
Log into your Bay account or “Check Order Status” (order number and postal code required if you placed your order as a guest.)

Logged in to your Bay Account?

- Go to “Order History” and choose the order with the product you wish to return

- Once submitted, the seller will contact you.

- Get your order number and postal code ready
