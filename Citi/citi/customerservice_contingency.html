Overview

Citigroup has a comprehensive continuity / disaster recovery plan designed to meet both business and system requirements including daily back up and/or replication to recovery facilities in a real-time mode for all vital records, computer systems and data. Citigroup's Business Continuity Planning program is based on Federal Financial Institution Examination Council guidelines. Disaster simulation testing is mandated, documented and conducted regularly to ensure effective continuity for all infrastructure requirements. Citigroup Global Prime Brokerage, along with all business areas, has a documented recovery plan which covers various contingency scenarios.

  • The Prime Brokerage continuity plan has identified all critical systems and business functions and developed plans to enable our systems and functions to be resumed in a timely order in the event of a disruption.
  • The Prime Brokerage Business Recovery plan includes:
    • Seating and double phone capacity for our staff at our contingency site
    • Call forwarding to contingency site upon any service interruption at Prime Brokerage headquarters at the Greenwich Street complex
    • Operations / technology support on site and adjacent to the Prime Brokerage group
    • Staff notification processes including an automated
    • messaging system and dedicated toll-free staff number.
  • Business Continuity plans including all necessary client data is updated quarterly
Business Recovery Activation Plan
  • Prime Brokerage senior management will be notified by the Business Continuity team in the event of any building outage
  • Prime Brokerage management will contact department managers
  • Employees will be contacted via our automated voice system and designated teams will be directed to our recovery site
  • Prime Brokerage disaster recovery / backup facility will be activated and immediately accessible to vital records, computer systems and data

Information Security

The ongoing security of our worldwide networks and data is coordinated, managed and supported by a vast number of system and technology teams throughout the Citigroup universe including our own Prime Brokerage technology teams. The Information Security initiative has mandated procedures for all Citigroup business areas as well as technology teams. The Citigroup Global Prime Brokerage business has designated a Business Information Security Officer who has implemented the following procedural mandates:

  • Risk assessment of all Prime Brokerage systems
  • Ongoing individual password security
  • Confidential disposal requirements
  • Awareness requirements including physical security
  • desktop, laptop security, anti-virus protection, personal firewalls, wireless security and clean desk policy.
